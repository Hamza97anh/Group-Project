Mental Health in The Tech Industry

What is our mission?

The Goal of this project is to use machine learning to predict the probeblity of an individual having a mental disorder in the tech industry. The model uses data pulled from the Open Source Mental Illness (OSMI)'s surveys from the years 2017, 2018 and 2019. Each survy includes a vast array of questions regarding not only potential causal factors that may lead to mental disorders, but also some quality of life issues that workers in the tech industry are facing today.

Machine Learning Model Results

Machine Learning Model:

We chose a logistic regression for our machine learning model as our desired dependent variable output is categorical and binary: whether or not a variable can predict the presence of a mental health disorder diagnosis for individuals in the tech industry (1 or 0). Such a model has the potential to provide correlations that could significantly impact the understanding of mental health disorders as they relate to the tech industry. However, we are also limited by the binary output of the algorithm. There are many different mental health disorders and providing only a yes or no output does not fully capture the story. If we had more time, we would have loved to attempt a multiple categorical output regression that is able to predict which mental health disorder an individual in the tech industry might have been diagnosed with. This could prove to be very beneficial in determining what potentially causes such disorders with employees in the tech industry.

Conclusion.:

Based on our data, there is definantly a large sum of individuals who work in tech that have a Mental illness, but being in that industry doesn't show to have a causal effect. As far as predicting Mental illness in someone, with our limited data it would seem like having a family history of mental illness coupled with being a women or somone who is non binary are more likely to have a mental illness. At least that would be the case in the tech industry. We can draw some specualtions as to why that is, there is certainly more than one possible reason for that, but that goes beyond this project.
